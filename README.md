# Skool.
Welcome to Skool. Where you go to school. In a unity game.
## Overall details
This is a school sandbox game.
### You can be a good student and...
* Chat with other students
* Go to class
* Be a teachers pet
* Get good grades
### Or you could be a bad student and...
* Escape school
* Explore the school
* Be a menace
* Get straight Fs
Your choice.
